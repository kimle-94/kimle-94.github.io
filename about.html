HEYYYYYYYYY! It’s nice to see you here again ✿
I’m Kim Le, a junior Product Designer based in Toronto. I have a passion for designing digital experiences that bring a positive impact on human.

I apply rigour and creativity, combining expertise in usability research, human-computer interaction, and graphic design to solve diverse design issues. I focus on interaction and minimalist design to improve the interactive experience between the user and the product.

Outside of design, I enjoy walking in nature, taking photos with my film cameras, and practicing Taekwondo (that has taught me some practical skills I can apply to my design work, such as decision making, problem-solving, intense concentration, and patience as well as quickly adapting myself to new situations).

WHAT SETS ME APART


I always put users first

I consider what users expect by putting myself in their shoes. In turn, I can meet their needs at all times, which leads to greater satisfaction and long-term success.


Collaboration plays a significant role in a team

When working in a team, communication enables designers to expand their horizons by simply hearing other's ideas.


Dealing with disagreements or failures as a necessary step toward success

We cannot avoid disagreements over opinions when collaborating with others since everyone has different perspectives. Instead of trying to win, we should approach it as a constructive idea.
